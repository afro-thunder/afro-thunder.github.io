Youtube Poop

Youtube Poops (also referred to as YTPs) are a form of parody video published on the video sharing website Youtube. They first became popular in 2006, a year after Youtube's creation. They consist of clips and references edited from popular television programs, movies, and other pop culture.

History

The video considered to be the first Youtube Poop is "I'D SAY HE'S HOT ON OUR TAIL", which was originally posted to SheezyArt on December 22nd, 2004 by the user SuperYoshi under the title "Super Mario Bros 3 REMIXED!!!". It was later posted to Youtube on November 22nd 2006.
